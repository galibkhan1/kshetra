Youth Centric Campaigns

A youth centric campaign organised by Kshetra targets and engages budding young people, typically between the ages of 17-30.In a youth centric campaign we understand the target audience,in this case- the youth.Their interests, preferences, behaviours, digital habits, preferred communication channels and concerns are taken into consideration. Youth centric campaigns focus on issues that are apposite to the youth of one's nation-employment, education, mental health, technology, climate change etc. The campaign carefully addresses the young people and help reach out the necessary message to them in a clear and concise way.Use of creativity, humour and authenticity along with engaging videos and interactive sessions encourage participation and activism among the youth. We sincerely measure the impact that in turn helps determine its effectiveness and Signing petitions, attending events, volunteering or donating to good causes provide opportunities to the youth to get involved and make a impactful difference.

Woman Centric Campaigns

Creating effective woman-centric campaigns requires a deep understanding of the unique challenges and aspirations women face. Additionally, "Her Story Matters" could advocate for better representation of women in media, partnering with filmmakers to create content that reflects women's experiences.
