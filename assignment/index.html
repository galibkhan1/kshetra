ABOUT KSHETRA CONSUTING

Our Story

Kshetra is a non-profit, non-partisan political consulting organization, with operations across India. We work to strengthen Indian democracy by providing grassroots support, operational research, party cadre management, narrative & building, media communications, policy design, and support to legislators, bureaucrats, and political parties.​ Kshetra specializes in building strategy and working with state and national parties and leaders including Chief Ministerial candidates, ideologists, voluntary groups and change-makers.
Kshetra Consulting is initiated by Rishikesh Reddy an XLRI Alumini and later co founded by members from IIT- Madras, IIT- Delhi, IIM- A, with experience from World Bank, IPAC, State Governments. The founding members had worked on political & government assessment of India’s biggest states and suggested real time campaigns. The leadership team of Kshetra is a pioneer in political campaigns, Election Management, Policy Analysis, Governance Initiatives, Media Communications, and technology which emphasizes delivering risk intelligence, decision-making, and public governance. Kshetra specialize in building strategy and work with state and national parties and leaders including Chief Ministerial candidates, ideologists ,voluntary groups and change-makers.
